Model Notes

This simulator uses historical weekly or monthly returns to estimate volatility, correlation, drawdown behavior, and empirical regime transitions. The Monte Carlo engine samples correlated shocks, updates BTC collateral value, estimates mNAV compression, updates STRC price as a function of par-defense credibility and stress, consumes reserves when issuance is impaired, and allows forced BTC sales when reserve runway falls below the configured threshold.

mNAV(BTC holdings * BTC price - debt - preferred notional + cash) / common market cap proxy
Preferred obligationpreferred notional * dividend rate + debt * debt interest
Reserve runwaycash reserve / monthly obligation
Confidenceweighted score from mNAV, reserve runway, macro liquidity, BTC pressure, and recent drawdown
Issuance capacityconfidence * sigmoid(mNAV - danger + buffer)
STRC priceemergent price from required yield spread, confidence, par defense, and observed volatility
Spiral eventderived STRC severe break, mNAV stress, forced-sale runway, and risk-off regime coexist

This is not a recommendation engine. It is an assumption engine. The useful output is the set of conditions required to break the structure.
